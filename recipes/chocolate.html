Chocolate

Description

If you live in a tropical country, as I do, is probably that you have access to cocoa beans. During this recipe we will go through the process of turning this into bitter chocolate.

Ingredients

  • The cocoa beans from around 3 cacao fruits
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1 tbs of vanilla
  • 1 tbs of olive oil
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tbs of peanut butter

Special Materials

  • 1 bag of charcoal
  • A baking sheet
  • A grill
  • A chocolate mold
  • 2 sheets of wax paper

Steps

  1. Break the cacao fruits to extract the cocoa beans
  2. Put the cocoa beans over the wax paper sheets and let them ferment 7 days.
  3. After those days have passed, put them in the baking sheet and roast them in the grill.
  4. When they are ready, peel the skin out of every bean.
  5. Put all the materials in a blender and blend 3 times until you have a pasty consistency.
  6. Spread the result over the chocolate mold and let it freeze for around 3 hours.
  7. Take your chocolate out of the mold and there it is! A bar of chocolate made by yourself, you should be very proud.
*This ingredients are equivalent to 1 or 2 chocolate bars
