Milkshake

Description

A very nutritious and full protein milkshake that everyone can made at home. This is a healthy alternative if you want a sweet energy-full breakfast for you or your children, and the best of it is that is very cheap!.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana (Froze it in order to increase it sweetness)
  • 1/3 cup of rolled oat
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of peanut butter
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • Cinnamon to taste
